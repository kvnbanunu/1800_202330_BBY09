Track Your Contribution

Our app helps track users in contributing to a greener environment effortlessly. By tracking and rewarding users for choosing eco-friendly transportation options, mainly buses over cars, we aim to promote greener habits. For every journey taken on a bus, our app plants trees as a tangible and impactful way of reducing polution. Join us in fostering a collective commitment to environmental conservation, one bus ride at a time.

You Are Not Alone

Additionally, our app goes beyond individual efforts by fostering a sense of community. The inclusion of a leaderboard feature allows users to connect with peers and observe how their actions contribute to a greener environment. By showcasing the real-time impact of each user's eco-friendly choices, the leaderboard not only inspires friendly competition but also creates a shared sense of accomplishment. Together, we can turn environmental sustainability into a shared journey, encouraging one another to make a positive impact and collectively shape a more sustainable future.
